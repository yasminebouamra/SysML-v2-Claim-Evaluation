General Mass Limitation Requirements
- The actual mass should be less than or equal to the required mass
Vehicle Mass Limitation Requirements
- The total mass of a vehicle should be less than or equal to the required mass
- The total mass should be calculated as the sum of the vehicle's dry mass and fuel mass
- The vehicle fuel mass should be greater than 0 kg
Drive Power Interface Requirements
- The engine should transfer its generated torque to the transmission via the clutch interface
