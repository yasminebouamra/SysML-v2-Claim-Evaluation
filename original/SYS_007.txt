Aircraft Performance Requirements

- The aircraft should have a maximum range of at least 1000 km
- The aircraft should have a maximum endurance of at least 4 hours
- The aircraft should have a minimum velocity of at least 80 km/h
- The aircraft propeller should produce enough thrust to exceed drag
- The aircraft should have a stall velocity of at least 80 km/h with flaps
- The aircraft should have sufficient thrust available to travel at a greater velocity than the stall velocity

Aircraft Control and Navigation Requirements

- The aircraft should determine accurate position and velocity data
- The aircraft should accurately determine altitude and speed
- The aircraft should display accurate position and heading
- The pilot should be able to control the heading of the aircraft based on displayed data

Aircraft Stability Requirements

- The aircraft should have any features necessary for stable flight
