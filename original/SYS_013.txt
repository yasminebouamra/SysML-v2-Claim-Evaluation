Grass Cutting Operation Requirements

The lawnmower should perform grass cutting operations in the following sequence:
Start the engine
Push the mower
Shut off the mower



Component Assembly Requirements

The lawnmower should be comprised of the following components:
One engine
One blade
One deck
One front left wheel
One front right wheel
One rear left wheel
One rear right wheel
One handle



Component Connection Requirements

The engine should be connected to the deck
The engine should be connected to the blade
The deck should be connected to the front left wheel via a pin connection
The deck should be connected to the front right wheel via a pin connection
The deck should be connected to the rear left wheel via a pin connection
The deck should be connected to the rear right wheel via a pin connection
The deck should be connected to the handle

Engine Requirements
Engine Mass Requirement

The engine should have a mass of 190 units
[Commented requirement] The engine mass should be less than or equal to 200 units

Engine Material Requirements

The engine block material should be iron

Engine Operating Requirements

The engine should have a minimum operating temperature of 0 degrees

Engine Component Requirements

The engine should contain the following components:
One piston with a diameter of 1 unit and mass of 3 units
One connecting rod
One crankshaft with a mass of 5 units
One crankshaft bearing with a mass of 2 units
One engine block with a mass of 6 units



Engine Force Transfer Requirements

The piston should transfer force to the connecting rod through the piston port
The connecting rod should transfer force to the crankshaft through the connecting rod crank port
The crankshaft should transfer force to the crankshaft bearing through the crankshaft bearing port
The crankshaft bearing should transfer force to the engine block through the bearing block port
The engine block should transfer force back to the piston through the cylinder port

Deck Requirements

The deck should have a color specification of "Blue"
The deck should have a mass of 50 units

Blade Requirements

The blade should have a diameter of 24 units
The blade should have a mass of 5 units

Wheel Requirements
Wheel Assembly Requirements

Each wheel should consist of:
One rim
One tire



Rim Requirements

The rim should have a diameter of 5 units
The rim should have a width of 2 units
The rim should have a mass of 4 units

Tire Requirements

The tire should have standard tread
The tire should have a mass of 5 units

Connecting Rod Requirements
Rod Bolt Variant Requirements

The connecting rod should have two rod bolts with the following variant options:

Rod Bolt Variant A

Mass: 2.5 units
Length: 2 units
Material: Steel

Rod Bolt Variant B

Mass: 2.0 units
Length: 2 units
Material: Aluminum
